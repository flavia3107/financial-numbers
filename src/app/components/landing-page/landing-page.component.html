Welcome! This app helps you convert shorthand financial numbers into their full numeric forms.

Instructions

  1. Enter financial numbers in shorthand format (e.g. 250k, 10m, .5b) on the Input page.
  2. Navigate to the Output page to see the numbers expanded in full and their average.

Tip: Please enter only valid shorthand financial numbers. Examples: 250k, 10m, .5b.
